About Me

Hi, my name is Jasmine. I go to school for Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. I have always had a passion for photography and trying to keep up with the latest technology (I never did, I lived in the Country with no internet until I was 18). I decided to pursue a field that was filled with technology and a field where I would have many options in my program.

My program contains Graphic Design, UX Design, Photography, Videography, CMS, Web Design and Motion Graphics.

I feel like I do well in the majority of the different aspects of my program, however I do not feel like I excel in any, therefore I have been taking part in Free Code Camp and have been loving my journey so far.

Please join me on my journey of becoming the best Interactive Media Designer that I can be! (Yes that includes coding so please bare with me!)
